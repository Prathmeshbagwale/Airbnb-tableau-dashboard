## 📌 Project Overview

An **end-to-end exploratory data analysis and interactive dashboard** of AirBnB listing data, designed to simulate a real-world business scenario where a hospitality company needs to understand market pricing, location trends, and revenue opportunities.

Built using **Tableau Public**, this dashboard follows best practices in **UX design, data wrangling, and visual storytelling.**

---

## ❓ Business Questions Answered

- Which **neighborhoods and zip codes** command the highest average prices?
- How does **pricing vary by room type** (entire home vs private room)?
- What is the **revenue potential** across different listing categories?
- How do **reviews and ratings** correlate with listing performance?
- What are the **seasonal availability trends** for AirBnB hosts?

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

- 📍 Location is the **#1 driver of price** — zip code analysis reveals 3x price differences across areas
- 🏡 Entire home listings generate **significantly higher revenue** than private rooms
- 📅 Availability peaks correlate with **tourist season trends**
- ⭐ Higher review counts don't always mean higher price — quality matters more
